FOCI LAB

Laboratory of Functional Computational Imaging

Laboratory of Functional Computational Imaging (FOCI Lab, in preparation) was established in Nov 2022.

Research topic - FOCI means Laboratory of FunctiOnal Computational Imaging.

Research concept - FOCI means Freedom, Open, Cooperation, and Innovation

Research topic - FOCI FOCusing on cutting-edge perspectives and Identify human brains

Cooperative medical partners: including Wuhan Union Medical College Hospital, Wuhan University People’s Hospital, Shanghai Mental Health Center, You’an Hospital of Capital Medical University, Union Medical College Hospital affiliated to Fujian Medical University, etc.

Cooperative academic partners: including Harvard University, University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, North Carolina State University, etc.

焦点（FOCI）实验室（筹）Laboratory of Functional Computational Imaging于2022年11月成立，现研究团队主要成员与科研平台隶属于南京理工大学。

科研方向上，FOCI意为Laboratory of FunctiOnal Computational Imaging（功能计算成像实验室）

科研理念上，FOCI意为Freedom（自由），Open（开放），Cooperation（合作），Innovation（创新）

科研思路上，FOCI意为“焦点”，聚焦前沿视角，指点健康大脑

合作医疗伙伴：包括武汉协和医院、武汉大学人民医院、上海精神卫生中心、首都医科大学佑安医院、福建医科大学附属协和医院等

合作学术伙伴：包括哈佛大学、北卡罗莱纳州大学大学教堂山分校、北卡州立大学等

我们正在努力发起一项Connectome Atlas Project（CAP，帽子）计划，旨在构建最广泛的Atlas of Brain Connectome （ABC）
